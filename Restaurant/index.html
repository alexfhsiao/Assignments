Welcome to Blueberry Hill Cafe

Located on the bank of the Mississippi, Blueberry Hill Cafe is Saint Paul's first Michelin 3-Star restuarant. Founded in 1912, Blueberry Hill Cafe was the inspiration for Fats Domino's Blueberry Hill. We serve a wide range of farm-to-table brunch foods and home brewed beers.
